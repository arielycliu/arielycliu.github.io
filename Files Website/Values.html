My Values

My Values

Curiousity

I never want to forget the joy of learning. Pursuing knowledge and understanding not for some grand goal, but a reasonless intent, to learn for the sake of learning. Going deep down rabbit holes of knowledge, learning new skills, and innovating on difficult projects. Being openminded to learn and approach life with the same open mindset.

Figure it out

Being able to problem solve and work things out on your own. Being accountable for your own problems and goals, along with hustling to achieve them instead of waiting to be spoon fed with the proper information.

Bias to Action

Not constantly overthinking, but instead doing. Being willing to take risks without hesitation, instead of constantly weight the positives and negative naunces of the decision. Action leads to experience which provides more value.

Anti-Fragility

Anti fragility is one step beyond resilience, it represents growing from your struggles.This means having a growth mindset, and putting yourself in uncomfortable/risky situations to learn from. Being internally motivated for personal growth, and leveraging feedback properly.

On the Ball

Being organized and in control, you don't need to be reminded what needs to be done. You keep yourself in check, and you don't forget to do tasks.

Gratitude

I want to stay grounded, and not become entitled. Having gratitude is a reminder of why we have to give back. I don't have to take things for granted and ensure I uphold my responsibility.

Authenticity

This is still something I really need to work on. I want to be confident in who I am, like boss mentality. I want to have the courage to stand for who I am unapologetically.
