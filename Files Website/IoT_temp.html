WORKING PROCESS

How It Works
Collect & Temperature Data

I used a TMP36 temperature sensor to gather temperature data. The TMP is a analog sensor, with a range of -40 to 150C. Using my ESP I read the voltage returned from the sensor and calculated the corresponding temperature reading.

Connecting & to WiFi

Using the ESP32, a chip microcontroller designed for low power consumption at a low cost with IoT applications in mind. I can connect to my home WiFi network.

Sending Data to ThingSpeak

Once the ESP was connected to the home WiFi network I could also connect to the ThingSpeak API. I sent the temperature data to a pre-created channel, and can monitor the data in real-time.
