Identifying Trump’s Tweets with Neural Networks


Using a neural network to predict which tweets are from Trump, which are from Hillary, and which are from the White House Staff.

In order to recognize Trump’s tweets, we’ll be using neural networks. Neural networks can model complex relationships between variables and predict possible outcomes, meaning it’s the perfect tool for this job! Neural networks take inspiration from the neurons and behaviors of our brain, they’re composed of many artificial neurons chained together to generate complex behaviors.

WORKING PROCESS

How It Works
Collect & Clean Data

I was able to mine data from twitter by using the Twitter API. Neural networks can’t process text directly, I used a TFIDF Vectorizer to fit the text data.

Develop & Train Model

I feed the network the labeled tweet data (input data). The model calculates a prediction, compares it to the correct label, and makes adjustments to its weights and biases

Test the Model

Putting testing data into the algorithm the model to gives a prediction of who the tweet was from. Overall, it was able to differentiate tweets with a high accuracy score of around 85%
